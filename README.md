# PlaneGuessr

![PlaneGuessr Screenshot](https://assets.thefilip.com/planeguessr.jpg)

**PlaneGuessr** is a browser-based aircraft recognition game. Identify planes from side-profile illustrations across 60 aircraft from 13 manufacturers.

**[Play Now](https://planeguessr.com)**

## How It Works

You're shown a side-view illustration of an aircraft and pick the correct answer from multiple choices. Depending on difficulty, you'll guess the manufacturer, family, or exact model.

## Difficulty

| Level | You guess | Example |
|-------|-----------|---------|
| Manufacturer | Who made it | Airbus, Embraer, Bombardier |
| Family | Aircraft family | A320, 737, CRJ |
| Exact Model | Specific variant | 787-9, A350-1000, E190-E2 |

## Game Modes

- **Normal** — No time pressure, work through the full aircraft set
- **Timed** — Pick a time limit (15s–90s) and score as many as you can. Streaks give score multipliers

## Filters

Filter the aircraft pool before starting:

- By manufacturer (Airbus, Boeing, Embraer, Bombardier)
- By size (Widebody, Narrowbody)
- By role (Cargo, Passenger)

## Aircraft Covered

60 aircraft across 13 manufacturers: Airbus, Boeing, Embraer, Bombardier, ATR, McDonnell Douglas, Douglas, Lockheed, Saab, Sukhoi, COMAC, De Havilland Canada, and Aerospatiale/BAC.

Includes everything from the DC-3 and Concorde to the 777-9 and A330-900neo.

## Features

- Streak counter and score multipliers in timed mode
- Lifetime stats (games played, accuracy, best streak)
- Keyboard shortcuts (1–4 to quick-answer)
- Mobile-friendly responsive layout
- Dark theme
- No accounts, no tracking — stats saved locally
