Privacy Policy

planeguessr.com

Overview

PlaneGuessr is a free browser-based game. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. This policy explains what minimal data is handled locally on your device and what anonymous analytics data is collected through Cloudflare.

Data we do not collect

We do not collect, process, or store:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • IP addresses or location data
  • Device or browser fingerprints
  • Cookies of any kind

Cloudflare Analytics

This site uses Cloudflare Web Analytics, a privacy-focused analytics service. It collects anonymous, aggregated data such as page views, visitor counts, and general geographic region (country level). Cloudflare does not use cookies, does not track individual users across sites, and does not build advertising profiles.

This data is used solely to understand overall traffic to the site. You can read more about Cloudflare's approach in their privacy policy.

Local storage

PlaneGuessr saves your high score to your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

Aircraft illustrations

Aircraft illustrations used in this game are sourced from norebbo.com and are used for personal and educational purposes. If you are the creator and would like them removed, please contact us.

Children's privacy

PlaneGuessr does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in any meaningful way, the date at the top of this page will be updated.
